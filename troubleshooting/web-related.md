## I forgot my password

1. Go to the SKlauncher site, click **Sign in**, then [**Forgot password?**](https://skmedix.pl/reset-password).
2. Follow the on-screen instructions.

If your Discord account uses the same email as your SKlauncher account, you can also click **Login with Discord**. Useful for the rare case you've forgotten your email too (though Discord itself shows it under your account).

:::info
If you've forgotten your email **and** lost access to it, the account can't be recovered.

That's a good moment to start using a password manager (Bitwarden, KeePass, etc.) so this doesn't happen again.
:::
