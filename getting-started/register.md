# Register

:::info
Registering is **optional**. You don't need an account to try SKlauncher (see the [Log in](/getting-started/login) guide).

You **do** need one if you want to **customise your skin and cape**.
:::

## Sign up

1. Open the [SKlauncher site](https://skmedix.pl/) and click [**Sign in**](https://skmedix.pl/login).
2. Choose your sign-up method:
    - **Discord**: click *Login with Discord* and follow the prompts.
    - **Email**: click [**Register now**](https://skmedix.pl/register) and follow the prompts.
3. Done. Your SKlauncher account exists.

:::warning
Don't lose access to your Discord or email account. Without it, you can't recover your password.

> If you still have access but forgot the password, [it's recoverable](/troubleshooting/web-related#i-forgot-my-password).
:::

## Set up your profile

The account exists, but you still need a **profile**. That's what defines your in-game username and unlocks skin / cape customisation.

Head to the [Customisation page](https://skmedix.pl/settings/customization), follow the prompts, then [Log in](/getting-started/login) to the launcher.

> **Picking a username**: if it already exists on SKlauncher, you can't reuse it. Some users also run it through the [Xyrios name checker](https://xyrios.com/minecraft/tools/name-checker) to avoid clashing with a Microsoft account.
