# Web related

:::tip
Before you dig in here, check the [Downloading common questions](/getting-started/downloads#common-questions) first.
:::

## Can I migrate my Other Launcher account to SKlauncher?

Yes:

- [Create your SKlauncher account](/getting-started/register) and use the **same username** you had in the other launcher.
- It's case-sensitive. `TheExample` and `theexample` are not the same name.
- You won't lose anything because the UUID stays the same (unless the other launcher uses a different UUID system).

## How do I put on a skin or cape?

- Already have your own? Open [Customisation](https://skmedix.pl/settings/customization) and upload them. Pay attention to the **Slim** field, which affects the look.
- Still looking? Browse our [skins](https://skmedix.pl/skins) and [capes](https://skmedix.pl/capes) pages: thousands ready to use.

:::info
On a Microsoft account you'll need to upload via the [Minecraft Launcher](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/download) ([alternative download](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/download/alternative)). See [skins priority](#will-other-players-be-able-to-see-my-skin-and-cape) too.
:::

## Is it possible to upload transparent skins?

Minecraft: Java Edition doesn't support transparent skins by default. They won't be visible without specific mods.

SKlauncher doesn't ship a mod for this since we don't modify the game. If you want to try it, [pick a suitable mod](/faq/launcher-related#can-i-use-clients-mods-resource-packs-shaders-etc-in-sklauncher).

## Can I upload an animated cape?

Same answer as transparent skins. SKlauncher doesn't modify the game, and that's required for animated capes to show in-world.

No plans to change this. If you want to see an animated cape in-game, [pick a suitable mod](/faq/launcher-related#can-i-use-clients-mods-resource-packs-shaders-etc-in-sklauncher).

## Will other players be able to see my skin and cape?

Yes, when **everyone on the server runs SKlauncher**.

If you've changed your skin and your friends don't see it, check that it's visible in your account settings on our site. Then restart the game and the launcher.

:::info
Whether you use a Microsoft or Offline account, the priority for skins/capes is:

1. SKlauncher
2. Microsoft
3. Server-side mods or plugins (e.g. SkinsRestorer)
:::
