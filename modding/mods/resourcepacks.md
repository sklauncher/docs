# Installing Resource Packs

:::danger
Always download resource packs from trusted sources. Anything else can damage your system.
:::

Resource packs change textures, GUI elements, and similar assets. They work on **vanilla Minecraft**, no mod loader required.

## Steps

1. Download the resource pack. It should be a `.zip` file.

    > On macOS, the download may arrive as a folder. Compress it back into a `.zip` before continuing.

2. Open your `resourcepacks` folder inside `.minecraft`:
    - Windows: `%APPDATA%\.minecraft\resourcepacks`
    - Linux: `~/.minecraft/resourcepacks`
    - macOS: `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/resourcepacks`

    > If you set a custom [game directory](/faq/launcher-related#how-does-game-directory-work), look for `resourcepacks` there instead.

3. Drop the resource pack `.zip` into the folder.
4. Launch the game and go to **Options > Resource Packs**. Move your pack from **Available** to **Selected**.

That's it. If everything is correct, your textures should be applied.
